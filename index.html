Welcome

Make a splash in Blue Dolphin community pool! Join us for public and lane swimming at our facility.

Centrally located in downtown Ottawa, Blue Dolphin has an 50-metre warm water pool and a Kiddlie pool for small children, which offers regular aquatic programming and specialized programs for individuals with special needs. The facility also has a fully accessible weight room, five meeting rooms of various sizes and access to an adjacent school gym.

Blue Dolphin offers a variety of programs for seniors, persons with special needs, and swimmers. Through a partnership with the Green Leaf Center Association (GLCA), children's and adult programs are also available to the Centretown community. These programs include: an after school program, children's camps, an adult fitness program, and adult art, dance, yoga, and sports programs.
